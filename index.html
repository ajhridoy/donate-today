Our Donate Plan

We want to stand by the flood victims in a few ways. We want to work on food, shelter,
and the education of children in that area.

Food For a Family

Food is most needed during this flood so that everyone can sustain life. Since there is no option to cook, we will give them dry food..

Shelter For a Family

Many houses have been submerged in this flood. We will provide some shelter for them as they will not have a place to stay after the floods..

Education For a Child

In flood prone areas we will buy books for children to study so that they can continue their studies.
